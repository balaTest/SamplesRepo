Welcome to the Jungle!

Interpreting CSS can get tricky from time to time, but the best way to learn is to create some text, then apply styles, and start tweaking until you get things looking the way you want them to. Don't forget to check your work inside multiple Web browers, though: you may be surprised by differences between IE and Firefox, for example.

If you encounter inconsistencies, you may have to try something different to make your pages behave in more or less the same way inside different browsers.
